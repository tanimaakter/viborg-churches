Unless there is a church action, Viborg Cathedral
is normally open for your visit.

at 11.00 - 17.00, Sundays and public holidays at
13.00 - 17.00.

1 October to 30 April:
at 11.00 - 15.00, Sundays and public holidays 13.00 - 15.00.
